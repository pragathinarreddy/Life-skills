# Prevention of Sexual Harassment

## Question 1: What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?
- Inappropriate comments or jokes of a sexual nature
- Unwanted touching or physical contact
- Sending offensive messages or images
- Making repeated invitations after refusal
- Staring, gestures, or leering that makes someone uncomfortable
- Verbal remarks about someone’s appearance or body

## Question 2: What would you do if I face or witness such behaviour?
- Clearly tell the person to stop if safe to do so
- Document the incident (note time, place, people involved)
- Report the behaviour to HR or PoSH committee
- Share evidence like messages or screenshots if available
- Support the affected person and encourage them to report
- Follow up to ensure action is taken
