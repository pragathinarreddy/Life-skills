# Learning Process

## 1. Feynman Technique

### Question 1: What is the Feynman Technique?
- It is a method of learning where you understand a concept by explaining it in simple words as if teaching someone else.

---

## 2. Learning How to Learn (Barbara Oakley)

### Question 2: Most Interesting Idea
- The idea that struggling while learning is normal and helps the brain grow was very interesting to me.
- The concept of switching between focused and relaxed thinking also stood out.

### Question 3: Active and Diffused Modes of Thinking
- Active mode is when you focus deeply on a problem and try to solve it step by step.
- Diffused mode is when your brain is relaxed and makes connections in the background.

---

## 3. Learn Anything in 20 Hours

### Question 4: Steps to Approach a New Topic
- Break the skill into smaller parts
- Learn enough to self-correct
- Remove distractions
- Practice at least 20 hours

---

## 4. Reflection

### Question 5: Actions to Improve My Learning Process
- Study with full concentration
- Keep my phone on silent while working
- Practice deep work daily
- Take notes and review concepts regularly
- Try to explain concepts in my own words
- Focus on mastering fewer skills deeply
- Track my time to improve productivity
